About Invidious

Invidious is an open-source alternative front-end to YouTube. It lets you watch YouTube videos without ads, tracking, or needing a Google account. If one instance is slow or down, try another one from the list above. All instances connect to YouTube's servers but through a privacy-preserving layer.
